bringing you the best hiking spots in europe

Suggestions of hiking trails, personalised advices, safety tips and much more

Each trail is identified with a difficulty level, walking distance, average time length, vertical distance, popularity, safety level and type of terrain. There are also some personalised advices that you can count on, such as wheter you should take a tent with you or not, whether you'll find places to eat... You can filter the search of your trails by country, region, popularity or any other characteristic within trails.
